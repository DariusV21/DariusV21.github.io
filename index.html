About Me

Hello! I'm Darius, a passionate craftsman specializing in custom electronics projects. I create functional and beautiful pieces that blend traditional woodworking with modern design.

From custom electronic kits and interactive gadgets to practical tech solutions, I leverage 3D printing for rapid prototyping and creating durable enclosures. While electronics and 3D printing are my primary focus, I also incorporate small-scale woodworking to add a touch of craftsmanship and natural warmth to my projects.
