About This Project

A beautifully crafted wooden cabinet specifically designed to house and hide your router and networking equipment. This custom piece combines functionality with aesthetic appeal, ensuring your home technology remains organized and out of sight while maintaining optimal performance.

The cabinet features thoughtful design elements including ventilation holes for proper airflow, strategic cable management cutouts, and a hinged door for easy access to your equipment. Made from premium wood with a natural finish that complements any home decor.

The Build Process

This project began with a client's need to hide unsightly networking equipment without sacrificing WiFi performance. After measuring their router and modem, I designed a cabinet that provides adequate ventilation while keeping everything organized.

The construction involved carefully selecting wood with matching grain patterns, precision cutting for the ventilation holes, and hand-finishing each surface. The hinges were strategically placed to allow full access while maintaining a clean look when closed.

The result is a functional piece of furniture that seamlessly blends into the home environment while solving a common problem in modern households.
